Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AxelBase FAQ Schema Generator, a client-side web utility developed by AxelBase ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our tool to generate FAQ Schema markup. We operate on a zero-data-collection principle. Since everything runs entirely in your browser, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data. This policy ensures complete transparency for your trust.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our tool processes everything locally. We never collect:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Names, emails, phone numbers, addresses
  • Usage Analytics: IP addresses, browser data, location, device info
  • FAQ Content: Your questions/answers stay on your device only
  • Tracking Data: No Google Analytics, cookies, or pixels

100% client-side = zero server transmission.

3. How the Tool Works

When generating FAQ Schema:

  • Input Processing: Enter questions/answers → JavaScript validates locally using schema.org rules
  • JSON Generation: Creates JSON-LD markup in your browser
  • Download Only: Click "Download" → File saves to your device
  • No Uploads: Bulk CSV processing happens offline

Your FAQ data never leaves your computer. Temporary browser storage clears on reload.

4. Third-Party Services

Zero third-party integrations:

  • No ad networks, analytics, or tracking services
  • Open-source libraries (local/CDN) with no user tracking
  • Downloads use browser native features only

5. Cookies and Tracking

No cookies. No tracking. Stateless sessions. Your visit leaves zero footprint.

6. Data Security

Since we collect nothing, there's no data to secure. Protect yourself:

  • Secure Browser: Use updated Chrome/Firefox
  • Sensitive FAQs: Don't download on public WiFi
  • Local Files: Keep generated JSON secure

7. Children's Privacy

Not for children under 13. No knowing collection from minors.

8. International Compliance

GDPR/CCPA compliant by default (zero data = zero issues).
