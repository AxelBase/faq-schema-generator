What is FAQ Schema?

Published: October 15, 2025

FAQ Schema is structured data markup that tells search engines your page contains Frequently Asked Questions. When implemented correctly, Google displays these as rich snippets—expandable FAQ sections directly in search results. Studies show FAQ rich snippets increase click-through rates by 20-30%.

How FAQ Schema Works

Using JSON-LD format, FAQ Schema follows schema.org/FAQPage standards. Each question-answer pair becomes a clickable accordion in Google's SERPs, giving users instant answers without clicking through to your site.

Rich Snippet Example

  • Visual: Bold questions + expandable answers
  • Position: Above organic results
  • CTR Boost: 25% average increase
  • Eligibility: 2+ questions minimum

Business Benefits

FAQ Schema reduces bounce rates by answering queries instantly. E-commerce sites see 15% higher conversions. Local businesses gain 18% more calls. Content sites enjoy 22% longer dwell time. It's SEO's highest ROI tactic in 2025.

Who Should Use It?

  • E-commerce product pages
  • Service business websites
  • Blog posts with common questions
  • Landing pages for lead gen

Technical Requirements

Valid JSON-LD with "@type": "FAQPage". Questions: 10-150 characters. Answers: 20-500 characters. No duplicate questions. Mobile-friendly markup. Google's Rich Results Test must pass 100%.

FAQ

Does FAQ Schema guarantee rich snippets?

Google decides based on quality and relevance. 85% valid implementations appear within 7 days.

Can I use FAQ Schema on every page?

Yes, but only add relevant questions users actually ask.

How many FAQs for rich snippets?

Minimum 2, optimal 5-8 questions per page.
